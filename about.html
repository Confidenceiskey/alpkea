ABOUT US

Alpkea was born as an idea to help other businesses be more succeessful. We love helping other people and it is this passion that drives us forward to continually improve our services.

DAVID NOWAK

Company founder, David Nowak, originally comes from a technical engineering background. Along the way he has taught himself programming and more recently found his craft as a chatbot developer.

Through learning marketing and sales, he has combined his skills to move into the newly created market of Facebook Messenger Marketing. Together with providing Facebook Ads, this creates a very powerful combination. If that isn't enough, he is also able to do full web design.

ORIGINS OF OUR NAME

With David having roots from around the world, he thought it would be fitting that our company name reflects this.

ALP - This part of our name comes from the European Alps. This is a region where David was living at the time and would be the starting steps in creating Alpkea.

KEA - The last part is a tribute to New Zealand. More specifically a magnificant bird called the Mountain Kea. This is where David began his entrepreneurship journey and began to focus on developing the necessary skills to create a business.

It has been a long, tough road for David to get to this point, but he is excited about what the future holds and the impact Alpkea can make.
